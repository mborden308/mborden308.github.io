About Me

Photography has always been my way of connecting with the world around me. What started as a simple curiosity turned into a deep passion for capturing the beauty I see in landscapes, cityscapes, and everything in between.

Armed with my Nikon, I've spent countless hours chasing light — from the still waters of the Boundary Waters Canoe Area Wilderness to the glowing skyline of downtown Minneapolis. Every photograph I take is an attempt to share the feeling of being there in that exact moment.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me planning my next adventure, exploring new trails, or scouting locations for that perfect golden hour shot.

— Michael Borden
